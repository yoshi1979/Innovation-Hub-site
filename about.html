About the Hub

About the Microsoft Israel
Innovation Hub

A dedicated hub within Microsoft Israel, built to accelerate the country's most ambitious technology companies — at every stage, from seed to scale.

Our Mission

The Microsoft Israel Innovation Hub exists to bridge the gap between Israeli tech innovation and Microsoft's global platform, expertise, and commercial reach. We work with startups, scaleups, ISVs and partners to help them build better products on Azure, reach enterprise customers faster, and grow into global technology leaders.

Our Vision

To make Israel the most Microsoft-connected startup ecosystem in the world — where every ambitious founder has access to the same tools, infrastructure, and go-to-market power as Microsoft itself.
