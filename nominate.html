Why nominations matter

You're often the first to spot high-potential companies — through your accounts, field conversations, partner network, or investor contacts. Your nomination puts a company on our radar before they find us.

Every nomination is reviewed by Tamar Cohen and routed to the right Hub program lead. The company doesn't need to know you nominated them unless you want them to.

What makes a strong nomination

  • Clear Azure relevance or migration potential
  • Strong technical team (CTO / VP Eng background)
  • Validated product with real customers or traction
  • Strategic fit with Microsoft's commercial priorities
  • Openness to co-sell or Marketplace collaboration
  • Founder/CTO availability for Hub engagement

What happens after you submit

1
Hub team reviews within 3 business days and assesses program fit
2
Tamar or Maya reaches out to the company for a 30-min discovery call
3
You're notified of the outcome and whether the company joins the Hub
✓
If enrolled, you're introduced as the referring Microsoft contact — giving you credit and visibility on the account
