☁️ AWS-to-Azure Startup Assessment

Seed–B ⏱ 2 weeks STU · ISE

A structured 2-week deep-dive into your current AWS architecture, identifying Azure readiness gaps, risks, and migration opportunities. You leave with a concrete plan — not a generic slide deck.

Customer Outcomes

  • Target-state Azure architecture diagram
  • Azure landing zone blueprint
  • 90-day migration roadmap

Deliverables

  • Architecture assessment report
  • Azure landing zone blueprint
  • Cost comparison (AWS vs Azure)

What Happens

  • Week 1: Architecture discovery sessions with your engineering team
  • Week 1: Multi-cloud gap analysis — compute, data, networking, security, IAM
  • Week 2: Azure readiness assessment & target architecture design
  • Week 2: 90-day migration roadmap with prioritised workloads
  • Readout: Presentation to founders & engineering leadership

Who Should Apply

Seed to Series B startups currently running on AWS who are exploring Azure as their primary or secondary cloud. Most useful before a major infrastructure decision or fundraise.

"After the assessment, we had a clear picture of what it would take to move to Azure — and the confidence to present it to investors as a strategic advantage."

🏗️ First Landing Zone Sprint

Seed–B ⏱ 4 weeks STU · ISE

A 4-week hands-on sprint with Microsoft ISE engineers to set up your first production-ready Azure landing zone — with security, CI/CD, and infrastructure-as-code from day one.

Customer Outcomes

  • Production-ready Azure landing zone
  • IaC templates (Bicep / Terraform)
  • Security baseline & CI/CD pipelines

Deliverables

  • Landing zone code repository
  • Architecture diagram
  • Runbook for operations team

Sprint Phases

  • Week 1: Requirements & design — networking, identity, policies
  • Week 2: Core landing zone deployment with ISE engineers
  • Week 3: Security baseline, monitoring, CI/CD integration
  • Week 4: Testing, runbook creation, team knowledge transfer

Who Should Apply

Seed to Series B startups at the beginning of their Azure journey — whether migrating from AWS, another cloud, or greenfield. You need at least one engineer who can participate in the sprint.

"We went from zero Azure infrastructure to a production-ready landing zone with IaC in four weeks. The ISE team treated it like their own project."

Customer Outcomes

  • Multi-cloud posture strategy
  • Azure expansion hypothesis
  • Risk assessment & 90-day roadmap

Deliverables

  • Assessment report (exec + technical)

  • MLOps framework on Azure AI Platform
  • AI governance baseline

  • Production architecture diagram
  • AI ops runbook

🏭 SaaS Modernization Studio

Mature ISV ⏱ 8 weeks ISE · STU · CSU

The most comprehensive modernization engagement in the hub portfolio. An 8-week studio combining assessment, architecture design, and a phased transformation plan — with Microsoft ISE, STU, and CSU working alongside your engineering team.

Customer Outcomes

  • Modernization scorecard across 12 dimensions
  • Target architecture on Azure
  • Phased transformation roadmap

Deliverables

  • Modernization report (exec + technical)
  • Architecture blueprints (current + target)
  • Roadmap with milestones & owner assignments

Studio Structure

  • Weeks 1–2: Technical discovery — architecture interviews, code & infra review
  • Weeks 3–4: Modernization scoring — 12-dimension maturity assessment
  • Weeks 5–6: Target architecture design workshops with ISE team
  • Weeks 7–8: Transformation roadmap, milestone planning, exec readout

Who Should Apply

Mature ISVs with legacy SaaS architectures (typically 5+ years old) experiencing scale limitations, technical debt, or competitive pressure. Best suited for companies with $10M+ ARR and a CTO willing to commit to the full 8-week engagement.

"The Modernization Studio gave us something we'd tried to build internally for two years — a credible, prioritised transformation plan that our board could fund and our engineers could execute."
