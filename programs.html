🚀 LaunchPad

LaunchPad is designed for Israel's most ambitious seed and pre-seed startups. If you're building on Azure (or planning to), we provide the infrastructure, guidance, and technical access to move from prototype to production in record time.

Eligibility

  • Seed or pre-seed stage
  • Israeli-registered company or Israeli founding team
  • Building a technology product (software, hardware+software, or AI)
  • Annual recurring revenue under $1M
  • Committed to building on Microsoft Azure

What You Get

  • $150K in Azure credits over 6 months
  • Weekly 30-min CTO mentorship with Avi Shapiro or hub advisors
  • Access to Azure OpenAI Service, AI Studio, and Copilot APIs
  • Co-working space at Microsoft Israel HQ
  • Invitation to LaunchPad demo day
  • Introduction to Microsoft's early-stage investor network
  • Access to CTO Circle events

Program Journey

  1. Application & Screening
  2. Onboarding & Azure Setup
  3. Build & Mentor Phase
  4. Demo Day & Graduation

📈 Scale360

Scale360 bridges the gap between product-market fit and enterprise scale. If you're Series A or B with revenue traction, we help you turn Microsoft into your most powerful sales channel.

Eligibility

  • Series A or B with at least $2M ARR
  • Product built on or migrating to Azure
  • Israeli-registered or headquartered company
  • Dedicated technical team (5+ engineers)

What You Get

  • Co-sell activation with Microsoft's GPS field team
  • Azure Marketplace listing support + ISV incentives
  • ISE (Industry Solutions Engineering) co-building sprints
  • Monthly Scale360 cohort roundtables
  • BD introductions to Microsoft enterprise customers
  • Azure credits extension
  • Access to Microsoft's global Partner Center

🏢 Enterprise Bridge

Enterprise Bridge is for companies that have achieved significant scale and are ready to build something larger — a joint product, a deep integration, or a co-go-to-market motion with Microsoft's global enterprise sales engine.

Eligibility

  • $10M+ ARR

🤖 AI Innovation Lab

The AI Innovation Lab is a 4-week intensive program where hub companies get hands-on access to Microsoft's AI stack — Azure OpenAI Service, AI Studio, Copilot extensibility, and more — with direct guidance from Microsoft AI engineers.

Eligibility

  • Any hub company
  • Technical team member must attend
  • Pre-work: Azure account set up and basic AI familiarity preferred

What You Get

  • Week 1: Azure OpenAI foundations and API integration workshop
  • Week 2: AI Studio — fine-tuning, RAG, and evaluation
  • Week 3: Copilot extensibility and agents
  • Week 4: Build sprint + demo to Microsoft AI team
  • $25K Azure AI credits
  • Office hours with Microsoft AI researchers
  • Post-lab: ongoing AI architecture support

🔨 Co-Build Program

The Co-Build Program is Microsoft's most exclusive offering for hub companies. Selected companies work directly with Microsoft Product Groups to explore, design, and sometimes build joint products or deep integrations.

Eligibility

  • Proven product with strong market traction
  • Technology that complements or extends Microsoft's product portfolio
  • By invitation only from Microsoft Product Group
  • Executive commitment from both sides

What You Get

  • Engineering collaboration time
  • Joint product design sessions
  • Roadmap alignment and co-announcement opportunities
  • Access to Microsoft's design and UX teams

  • Co-marketing and co-PR opportunities
